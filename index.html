    Feature-rich Internal Navigation

    Internal navigation system that allows users to interact with the system using natural language and receive real-time navigation guidance through voice commands. This feature aims to make navigation within the university campus more intuitive and accessible.

    Crowd Density Information

    Image processing techniques, such as object detection, are employed to produce real-time crowd density information. This feature helps in efficient crowd management by providing insights into the density of people in specific areas of the campus, allowing for timely decisions and interventions.

    Personalized Recommendations

    The system uses Natural Language Processing (NLP) to analyze user feedback and provide personalized recommendations for specific services or food items within the university. This feature tailors suggestions based on user preferences and interactions, improving the relevance of the system's outputs.

    Integration of Machine Learning

    Machine Learning (ML) techniques are integrated to enhance user interactions and recommendations within the navigation system. ML refines information delivery by learning from user interactions and tailoring responses, contributing to the system's effectiveness.

    Augmented Reality (AR) Technology

    AR technology is used to overlay digital information onto the physical environment, providing users with real-time visual cues and navigational directions. This feature enhances the user's awareness of their surroundings, making navigation more interactive and dynamic.

    Literature Survey

    AR navigation is a critical component of the system, enhancing user experience and wayfinding. Notable work by Azuma et al. [1] defines the fundamentals of AR and highlights its potential in improving navigation and interaction in various contexts. People counting, a core function of the system, relies on image processing techniques. Research by Chen and Luo [2] offers insights into image-based people counting, presenting methods to enhance accuracy and efficiency in crowded environments. NLP and voice-based interaction contribute to the system's accessibility and user-friendliness. Jurafsky and Martin's work [3] on NLP provides a foundational understanding of language processing, enabling effective user-system communication. The system's crowd management component aligns with research in smart environments. Zanella et al. [4] explore smart cities and environments, shedding light on the significance of crowd management in public spaces. Real-time data processing and cloud integration are pivotal for the system's functionality. Casado-Vara et al.'s research [5] discusses cloud computing in the context of real-time data processing, providing valuable insights into system architecture. The mobile application's development for the Android platform finds support in research by Mishra et al. [6], who detail best practices in mobile app development and usability considerations.

    Research Gap

    Numerous Studies

    Significant amount of researches have been conducted on AR-based navigation, Natural Language Processing systems, voice based interaction systems, people counting methodologies, and image processing.

    Features for internal navigation systems

    Currently, there are not many mobile applications that provide internal navigation capabilities, and many of the navigation systems made for educational institutions lack internal navigation features. The proposed product provides users with features such as AR-based internal navigation, voice-based interactions with virtual assistant, knowing the availability of various locations that provide a certain service, and feedback-based suggestions.

    Number of mobile applications

    It has been found that there are not many mobile applications developed for providing internal navigation capabilities for universities. However, the need for such mobile applications is seemingly high.

    Research Problems

    AR Navigation Enhancement

    How can AR navigation be further enhanced to provide more accurate and context-aware guidance to users in complex indoor and outdoor university environments?

    Accurate People Counting in Crowded Spaces

    What techniques and technologies can be developed to ensure highly accurate people counting in crowded areas while addressing challenges like occlusions and varying lighting conditions?

    Finding the Best Canteen for a Food Item

    How can natural language processing (NLP) be integrated to create a more user-centric interaction, allowing the system to use feedback submitted by users to determine the best canteen for a specific food item?

    Contextual Understanding

    How can voice-based interaction systems be improved to better understand the context of user queries, allowing for more accurate responses and navigation instructions in diverse university settings?

    Main Objectives

    The main objectives was to develop an intelligent, user-friendly web platform that detects and explains various forms of digital misinformation-including deepfake videos, AI-manipulated images, fake news in English and Sinhala, and political bias in Sinhala media-using advanced machine learning and explainable AI techniques. The goal is to help users verify content more easily, promote digital literacy, and reduce the spread of misleading information across online platforms.

    English fake news and voice analysis system

    To use video feed to identify the number of people present at a certain location, and in turn provide users with the availability of each location.

    Sinhala fake news and political biased detection system

    To use feedback submitted by users in making better suggestions for questions users ask about certain services.

    Image analysis System

    To distiguish real genuine images from edited images and AI generated images.

    Deepfake Video Detection System

    To distiguish real genuine videos from videos subjected to deepfake alterations.

    Sub Objectives

    Enhanced User Navigation

    Provide users with seamless and user-friendly navigation within the university through Augmented Reality (AR) and voice-based interactions.

    Safety and Crowd Management

    Ensuring the safety of users by effectively managing crowd density in real-time, especially during emergencies and high-traffic events.

    Customization for Campuses

    Enabling the system's customization, allowing individual campuses to tailor the system to their specific needs, layout, and preferences.

    Real-Time Information

    Offering users real-time information on crowd density, location availability, and personalized recommendations to enhance their campus experience.
